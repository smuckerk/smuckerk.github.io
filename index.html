My background...

Hello! I'm Kennedy Smucker, an Honors student at the University of Michigan College of Literature, Science, and Arts. Growing up, I've always been driven to study science and medicine. My previous work experience spans from investigating anticancer properties of plant extracts as a Zoetis Research Assistant, to mentoring young students in STEM through teaching roles. Now, at the University of Michigan, I am continuing to deepen my understanding of biological science. I am eagerly searching for research positions and work related to the field of biology. My goal is to contribute to impactful scientific discoveries while making science more accessible for all communities.
